About Me

Having past experience in the IT industry, I enjoyed working with others to assist in issue resolution.
During that time, I worked with some self-taught coding, but it was not congruent. I have recently chosen to take on a Web Development Coding Bootcamp. I like the structured approach.

The Healthcare system requires software and IT technologies as a fundamental aspect of data security to maintain patient information protection (HIPPA).
More specifically, web application integration is increasingly important for the day-to-day charting activities for all personnel, training and proficiency materials distribution and Web-based continuing education.

As a Registered Nurse working in several hospitals, I have a unique perspective allowing for specialized input relating to the real-world needs of a healthcare professional in a never-ending technologically advancing industry. My objective is to combine my Healthcare and Web Development experience and contribute to easing the increasing complexity associated with technological advancements in industry.

Originally from the west coast, I now reside in the SLC, UT area.
Some hobbies I enjoy are playing guitar, fishing and riding my bike.

Being on an integral part of a dynamic and flourishing team to develop robust and secure web applications is what I am pursuing. I enjoy working on projects utilizing marketable skills I have obtained and realizing the good results therein.
